# Temporary Banner Placeholders

These SVG banners are designed to match your ACR Automotive brand colors and give you a preview of how professional banners will look in your carousel.

## Banner Files (1560x480px):

1. **banner-1-gradient.svg** - Bold red gradient with circular decorations
2. **banner-2-white.svg** - Clean white background with red accent stripe
3. **banner-3-dark.svg** - Dark background with red gradient box
4. **banner-4-split.svg** - Split design (red/gray) showing brand division
5. **banner-5-minimalist.svg** - Minimalist with large watermark and red accent

## How to Use:

1. Go to **Admin → Settings → Branding & Assets**
2. Scroll to **Banner Carousel** section
3. Click **"Add Banner"** for each banner you want to add
4. Upload the SVG files as **Desktop Images**
5. Leave **Mobile Images** blank (or upload same file)
6. Mark as **Active** and click **Save**

## Brand Colors Used:

- **ACR Red**: `#ed1c24` (primary brand color)
- **Dark Red**: `#d91920` (hover states)
- **Dark Gray**: `#111827` (text)
- **Medium Gray**: `#4b5563` (secondary text)
- **Light Gray**: `#f8f9fa` (backgrounds)
